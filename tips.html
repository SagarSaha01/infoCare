Mental and Physical Health Tips and Tricks

Improve Your Mental and Physical Health While in Isolation!


Doctors won’t make you healthy. Nutritionists won’t make you slim. Teachers won’t make you smart. Gurus won’t make you calm. Mentors won’t make you rich. Trainers won’t make you fit. Ultimately, you have to take responsibility. Save yourself.


Physical Health Tips:

Steps to improve your physical health.

Here are some tips for you to improve your physical health:

  1. Drink more water and fewer sugary drinks
  2. Excercise regularly
  3. Eat more fruits and vegetables and less fast food
  4. Get a good night's sleep
  5. Wash your hands
  6. Take time to relax
  7. Enjoy the present
  8. Get enough sleep

Here are some tips for you to improve your mental health:

  1. Get plenty of sleep
  2. Meditate
  3. Avoid alcohol, smoking and drugs
  4. Get plenty of sunlight
  5. Manage stress
  6. Do things that you enjoy
  7. Connect with others and socialize
  8. Relax in a warm bath once a week
